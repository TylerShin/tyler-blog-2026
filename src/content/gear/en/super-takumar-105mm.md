Shooting with a manual lens, stitching every shot together by hand, might seem inconvenient to some. However, the "tactile feel" is undeniable. After using it for a day, you start to get a feel for how much to turn the ring to get focus, and your shooting speed actually improves. (Of course, if you don't use it for a few days, you lose that touch immediately lol.)

Why not try slowing down and savoring the moment between you and your subject with a manual lens?
